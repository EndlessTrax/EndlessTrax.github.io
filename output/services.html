Services

Consulting services can often be difficult to quantify, as each client’s needs will be different. But my aim is to help martial arts business owners reach their goals, which may include:

  • Enrolling more new students
  • Retaining students
  • Becoming a more successful and profitable martial arts school
  • Growing the business
  • Learning about martial arts marketing and social media
  • Planning for the future

It may be that you are a new martial arts school just starting out, or one that has plateaued and needs a boost. Whatever the situation, I can help.

My consulting and coaching services focus on getting to the core of any issues, by spending time with you, the owner, and discussing the business in-depth. My role is not to fix any issues, but to explain how you can resolve any weak areas within the business, therefore providing a long term solution.

I want you to consider me your trusted business consultant who will provide honest advice, information, business support, and marketing knowledge.

Whilst I am based in the Washington D.C. Metro Area, I have two types of consulting services that can be carried out anywhere in the U.S.:

  • Hourly consultations via phone/video call
  • Residential consultations

Residential consultations involve me traveling to your location and spending a day/s at your school. By training with you and ‘shadowing’ you and your team, I can get a good overview of the school and highlight any issues that need to be addressed. A written report will be provided after the residential to summarize the findings and make recommendations for business improvements.

If you’d like an initial chat about how I can help your martial arts business, please contact me.
